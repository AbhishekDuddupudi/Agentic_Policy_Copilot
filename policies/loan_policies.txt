Loan Approval Policy

1. Purpose
This policy describes the criteria we use to evaluate and approve customer loan applications.

2. Eligibility Criteria
Applicants must:
- Be at least 18 years old.
- Provide valid government-issued identification.
- Provide proof of income for the last 3 months.
- Meet minimum credit score requirements as defined by our risk team.

3. Documentation
Required documents include:
- Recent pay stubs or bank statements.
- Photo ID (passport, driverâ€™s license, or national ID).
- Address verification (utility bill or bank statement).

4. Income and Affordability Assessment
We assess:
- Monthly net income.
- Existing debts and obligations.
- Debt-to-income ratio.
- Employment stability.

5. Decision Process
- Applications are initially scored using an automated credit model.
- Borderline or special cases may be escalated to a manual underwriter.
- Final decisions are communicated via email or the online portal.

6. Appeals
Applicants can request a review of declined applications by submitting additional documentation within 14 days.

For full details or edge cases, please refer to the internal underwriting guidelines.

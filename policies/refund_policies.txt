Refund Policy

1. Eligibility
Customers are eligible for a refund within 30 days of the original purchase date.
A valid receipt or order ID is required.

2. Refund Methods
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method.
We do not provide cash refunds for credit card purchases.

3. Non-Refundable Items
The following items are not eligible for a refund:
- Personalized or custom products
- Gift cards
- Products marked as "final sale"

4. Process
To request a refund:
- Contact support with your order ID and reason for refund.
- Our team will review the request within 3–5 business days.
- If approved, the refund will appear on your statement within 7–10 business days.

5. Partial Refunds
In some cases (e.g., partially used services), partial refunds or store credit may be offered at our discretion.

If you have questions about this refund policy, please contact support.

Customer Data Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect
We collect:
- Contact information (name, email, phone number).
- Account information (user ID, login history).
- Transaction data (payments, refunds, loan applications).
- Support interaction history (tickets, calls, chat transcripts).

2. How We Use Data
We use customer data to:
- Provide and improve our services.
- Process payments and refunds.
- Review loan applications.
- Investigate and resolve support issues.
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Retention
We retain data only as long as necessary for:
- Providing services.
- Meeting legal, tax, and compliance requirements.
- Handling disputes and audits.

4. Data Sharing
We may share data with:
- Payment processors.
- Credit bureaus (for loan and risk assessments).
- Regulatory authorities when required by law.
We do not sell customer data to third parties.

5. Customer Rights
Customers can:
- Request access to their data.
- Request correction of inaccurate information.
- Request deletion where legally permitted.

For any privacy-related questions, contact the Privacy Office or Support.
